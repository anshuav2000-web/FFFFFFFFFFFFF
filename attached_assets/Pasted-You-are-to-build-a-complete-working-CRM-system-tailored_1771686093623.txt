You are to build a complete, working CRM system tailored to a creative marketing agency called “Canvas Cartel”. The CRM should be functional, web-based, and loaded with practical features used by a marketing agency. It must include the following modules and functionality:

1. **User Authentication & Roles**
  - Admin login, Manager login, Sales/Account Executive login, and Staff roles with secure access.
  - Role-based dashboards.

2. **Lead & Contact Management**
  - Capture leads from website forms, email, and manual entry.
  - Auto-enrich leads (company, title, social links).
  - Lead source tracking.
  - Store contact details, conversation history, and tags for segmentation.

3. **Sales Pipeline & Deal Tracking**
  - Custom pipeline stages (e.g., New Lead → Contacted → Proposal Sent → Negotiation → Won/Lost).
  - Visual Kanban board for deals.
  - Deal value, expected close date, and probability scoring.

4. **Client & Project Management**
  - Active client list with project statuses (Onboarding, Design, Execution, Review).
  - Attach timelines, tasks, briefs, deliverables, and milestones per client.
  - Link proposals, assets, and contract docs.

5. **Automations & Notifications**
  - Send automatic email notifications for new leads, follow-ups, and task deadlines.
  - Reminder alerts and due date notifications for team members.

6. **Communication Logging**
  - Log calls, WhatsApp chat summaries, emails, Zoom/Meet meeting notes.
  - Auto-generate AI summaries for each interaction.

7. **Proposals & Contracts**
  - Template library for proposals and contracts.
  - E-signature support.
  - Track proposal views, acceptance and signed dates.

8. **Reporting & Analytics**
  - Dashboard with graphs: lead sources, sales conversions, revenue forecast.
  - Client retention reports, campaign performance, and monthly MRR/ARR.
  - Exportable PDF and Excel reports.

9. **Task & Team Management**
  - Assign tasks to team members with deadlines and priorities.
  - Global activity feed and team chat notifications.

10. **Integrations**
  - Integrate with Gmail/Outlook for email logging.
  - Calendar sync (Google/Outlook), WhatsApp Business, and Instagram/Facebook messaging.
  - Payment integration to track invoices and retainers.

11. **Mobile Responsive UI**
  - Clean, modern dashboard built with responsive design.
  - Support for mobile and desktop usage.

12. **Security & Backup**
  - Role-based access control, GDPR compliance, encrypted storage, and automated daily backups.

13. **AI Features**
  - AI assistant to generate:
    - Follow-up email drafts
    - Lead summaries
    - Active client health scores
    - Weekly performance insights

Your output should include frontend UI components, database schema, API routes, automation logic, user workflows, and security rules. Deploy the system ready to use (hosted environment or exportable source code).
